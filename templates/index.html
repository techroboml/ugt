About Us


UCHHWAASA GENERAL KNOWLEDGE TEST ( UGT ) - A Thread of Development in Direaction of Education.

Mainly it has been developed to empower the hidden talent of Rural students in the verenda of Competitive exams.

Each and every students of High Schools and UG Colleges will be beneficial by this Exam.

Since 2017 it has been working in the various schools and colleges by organizing two Modes of examinations
in every year bearing junior Round for High School Students (Class 8 th / 9 th / 10 th) and Senior Round For UG Students (+2 / +3 ).

We hope this UGT will bring a new Revolution for those Students in the chapter of Education.
